WHAT IS DOMESTIC VIOLENCE?

A one-time to pattern of violence whether being controlling or coercive tactics committed by one partner against another that consists of physical, psychological, verbal or sexual assaults mainly caused by abuser wanting power and control over the victim.

Consider the following characteristics as Red Flags that your relationship may be abusive

CONTROLLING BEHAVIOR | ISOLATION | USE OF FORCE | BLAME | HYPERSENSITIVITY | DISRESPECT
